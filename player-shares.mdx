* **Starting Price:** All players launch at **$0.01 per share** on a bonding curve (automated pricing system).  
* **Total Supply:** Fixed at **8,000,000 shares** per player.  
* **Initial Circulating Supply:** About **2,000,000 shares** available at launch (through packs, tournaments, and trading).  
* **Unvested Supply:** The remaining **\~6,000,000 shares per player** are reserved for future rewards, growth, and programs (e.g., new tournaments or quests).
