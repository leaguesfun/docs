---
title: "League XP Tiers"
---

Your total LXP earned over the season places you in a tier, from **Bronze** to **Diamond**. Higher tiers mean bigger bragging rights and better chances for the **\$LEAGUE airdrop**.

* **Bronze:** 0–50,000 LXP

* **Silver:** 50,001–250,000 LXP

* **Gold:** 250,001–1,000,000 LXP

* **Platinum:** 1,000,001–3,000,000 LXP

* **Diamond:** 3,000,001\+ LXP
